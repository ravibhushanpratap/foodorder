Prawn recipe

This post is written by Vishnu Vishesh

While this was good....it was also very one note, so after eating a helping I felt like it needed something fresh or an acid or something. I then went back for a second helping and added toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes and black olives, it was better then. If I make it again, I will leave the tortillas whole for more of a "lasagna" affect and add canned tomatoes like other have suggested. Or leave the tortillas out completely and make it with rice and then serve it with tortillas on the side. The flavors were good though.
